Purpose

  • Provide a model parenting plan form for consideration by parents, lawyers, and judges.
  • Suggest a time-sharing schedule based around the best interest of each child.
  • This website does NOT provide any legal advice.

Using the Website

The parenting plan details most information about child custody, and the general time sharing schedule details the time-sharing between the parents based on a typical school schedule.

  • Parenting plan form: A plan to be submitted to the judge detailing physical custody type of the child, information sharing protocol, transportation, and a myriad of other decisions affecting how the child will be raised. The time-sharing schedule is part of the parenting plan.
  • General time-sharing schedule: Part of the parenting plan; details how the parents will schedule time with children during the general school year. The General Time-Sharing Schedule does not detail how parents will schedule time with children during school holidays and summer breaks. The parenting plan form details how parents spend time with parents during school holidays and summer breaks (Holiday and Summer Time- Sharing Scheduling). An example of a general time-sharing schedule: parents alternate physical custody of the children each week.
